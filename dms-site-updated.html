Who We Are

Digital Music Systems ("we," "us," "our") provides jukeboxes, ATMs, amusement games, dart leagues, and trivia services throughout Washington and Oregon. DMS, Inc. is the trade name we use for our ATM business. This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through our website, dmswa.com, and through communications with us.

Information We Collect

When you fill out our contact form, we collect the information you provide: your name, business name, phone number, email address, and the details of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry, provide our services, and communicate with you about your account or equipment.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to inquiries, schedule service and installations, process quotes, and keep you informed about services you've requested. We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.
